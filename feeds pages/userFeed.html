Into the Future

VirtualBox is a free and open-source hypervisor for x86 computers that is developed by Oracle. It can be installed on a number of host operating systems, such as Linux, macOS, Windows, Solaris and OpenSolaris. It also supports the creation and management of virtual machines which run versions of Windows, Linux, BSD and others, as well as limited virtualisation of macOS guests on Apple hardware.

VR for Everyone

The Oculus Rift S offers a high-end VR experience without much fuss, striking a compromise between a more casual headset like the PlayStation VR and spec-heavy options like the Valve Index. The Rift S has the easiest physical setup of the major PC VR systems, with just one cord that connects the headset to the computer. Sensors can track your location in the room and your hand movements.
