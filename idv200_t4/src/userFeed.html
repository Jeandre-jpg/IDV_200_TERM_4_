Monitors for all

By Nadeem O'Ryan

This Dell UltraSharp monitor delivers a great balance of size, price, and performance. Its large 27-inch screen might even rival the monitor in your office, while the sleek stand won’t take over too much desk space. With a 16:9 aspect ratio, 2560 x 1440 resolution, and 60Hz refresh rate, the display offers great performance at a reasonable price for its size and capability. Plus, it’s hard to beat its adjustable stand, thin bezels, variety of ports, and three-year limited warranty.

Into the Future

By Usamah Mccormick

VirtualBox is a free and open-source hypervisor for x86 computers that is developed by Oracle. It can be installed on a number of host operating systems, such as Linux, macOS, Windows, Solaris and OpenSolaris. It also supports the creation and management of virtual machines which run versions of Windows, Linux, BSD and others, as well as limited virtualisation of macOS guests on Apple hardware and other Apple products.
